## LOCKED — Do not change without Dayna's explicit approval

**Core rule:** No signature at intake = no pickup. Agreement acceptance at booking is what opens the Handshake (per-client account/record). This protects Dayna from absorbing the cost of a free concierge pickup for someone who later disputes the terms.

## The 9-Step Handshake Flow

1. **INTAKE** — Agreement signed at bag-pickup request. Opens the Handshake account. Max 2 bags per non-session pickup.
2. **DAY-BEFORE EMAIL** — Confirms pickup window + asks rough item count.
3. **CUSTODY TRANSFER** — In-person release / UPS delivery / Uber courier. Pickup photo taken regardless. This legally transfers possession.
4. **48-HOUR INVENTORY CONFIRMATION** — Dayna verifies count against client's stated number. Client has 48 hours to flag discrepancies.
5. **7–10 BUSINESS DAY EVALUATION** — Viability, platform routing, pricing, estimated sale price, turn, and tier.
6. **FULL INTAKE REPORT** — Every item: platform, starting price, est. sale price, est. turn, alternate pathways. Biohazard/unsanitary items disposed immediately, no discussion.
7. **24-HOUR CONSENT WINDOW** — Client signs consent to listings OR pulls items. No response within 24 hours = listings proceed.
8. **4-DAY REVIEW PERIOD** — Client adds provenance, sizing, measurements, history to boost listings.
9. **30-DAY PAYOUT CLOCK** — Starts at consent signature (Step 7). First payout: first Monday after the 30-day close.

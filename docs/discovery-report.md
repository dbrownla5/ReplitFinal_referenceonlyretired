## Key brand elements discovered
- **Locked hero:** "Professional problem solver. Unofficial fixer of the things life keeps turning into projects."
- **Category:** practical operational support for modern life. Explicitly NOT: luxury concierge, home organization, lifestyle curation, caregiving.
- **Core differentiator (newest, from 6/12 sessions):** the skill is **judgment** — the act of evaluation itself, not the thing being evaluated. Must thread through ALL four pillars, especially Reset and House Calls where it's least obvious.
- **Emotional architecture:** notice reality → remove shame → introduce relief.
- **Four pillars:** Reset (Home Org & Modern Move) · House Calls · Curated Resale & Consignment · Legacy Planning & Inventory. Plus entry offers, Flex Blocks, bundles, projects, retainer.

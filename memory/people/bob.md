# Bob

**Role:** Customer from Dayna's retail days (Missouri store, Tuesday afternoon, fanny pack). Bought nothing.

## Why he matters to the brand
- The team saw a guy wasting time; Dayna saw a relationship. He told everyone at the barbecue; sent referrals for a month; took a photo with her.
- Same skill as the fur coat, the fake Gucci, the inventory calls: **assessing what something is actually worth when everyone around sees it differently.**
- Bob = the calibration point for SOCIAL voice: warm, observational, no polish, finds value others miss.
